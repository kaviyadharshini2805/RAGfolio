## Tips for Writing Your Resume Data

1. **Be Specific**: Use numbers, metrics, and concrete examples
2. **Use Action Verbs**: Built, developed, implemented, led, designed
3. **Show Impact**: Always include the result or impact of your work
4. **Keep It Current**: Update with your latest skills and experience
5. **Be Honest**: Only include skills and experiences you actually have
6. **Format Consistently**: Use the same structure across all entries
7. **Include Context**: Explain what, why, and how for each item

## Questions the Chatbot Can Answer

After filling in your data, the chatbot can answer questions like:

- "What are your main programming languages?"
- "Tell me about your most recent work experience"
- "What AI/ML projects have you worked on?"
- "Do you have experience with cloud platforms?"
- "What's your educational background?"
- "What databases have you worked with?"
- "Describe a challenging project you completed"
- "What are your strongest technical skills?"
